IMC Strategy Launch Planning

Project Overview

A comprehensive marketing analytics project focused on improving campaign performance and ROI through data-driven insights and strategic planning. In this project, I worked with my team to develop the marketing strategy for a hypothetical brand — Dark Side. We planned the strategy in three distinct phases: pre-launch, during launch, and post-launch.

Launch Phases

Pre-Launch

  • Buzz Marketing
  • Social Media Marketing
  • SEO
  • Influencer Marketing
  • Offline Marketing

During Launch

  • Launch Event
  • Influencer Marketing
  • PR
  • Media
  • Event Marketing

Post-Launch

  • PR
  • SEO
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Word of Mouth
  • SME

Key Elements

Market Research

Research about the cost involved in every step of the marketing, selecting the media channels, target audience, and the positioning of the brand.

Strategy

As we were positioning the brand as a premium brand, every strategy was crafted accordingly to maintain the premium positioning.

Performance Metrics

Development of KPIs and performance metrics to track campaign success and ROI.

Strategic Insights

Generation of actionable insights to optimize marketing strategies and improve outcomes.
