Project Overview

In this project, I developed a comprehensive business plan for a sustainable fashion brand, conducting market research, financial analysis, and creating a detailed marketing strategy.

Key Elements

Market Analysis

Conducted thorough market research to identify target audience, competitors, and market trends in the sustainable fashion industry.

Financial Planning

Developed detailed financial projections, including startup costs, revenue forecasts, and break-even analysis.

Marketing Strategy

Created a comprehensive marketing plan focusing on digital channels and sustainable brand messaging.

Stakeholder Management

Identified and engaged with key stakeholders, including potential investors, suppliers, and partners.
